Expand your résumé at Washington State!

Participate in summer research programs hosted by WSU faculty and funded by NSF Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU), USDA, and faculty grants at Washington State University located in the Pacific Northwest. You will:

  • Conduct research in cutting edge facilities
  • Be mentored by award-winning professors
  • Receive a generous stipend with many expenses covered
  • Showcase your work to the public at our poster symposium
  • Showcase your work to the public at our poster symposium
