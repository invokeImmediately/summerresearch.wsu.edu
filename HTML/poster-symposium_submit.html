Abstract Submission Form 2020

Use this form to submit your abstract for this year's Summer Undergraduate Research Virtual Symposium. Note: the submission deadline is Wednesday, July 15, 2020.

(Note: * indicates a required field. Please contact our web coordinator if you need any assistance with your submission.)

  • Your co-authors are those people who contributed to the research you are presenting at the symposium. They are the ones who deserve credit along with you for the advancement in knowledge that your research represents.

    • Enter one name per row in First Last format, e.g. Jordan Smith. Use the plus button situated at the right of each row to add an additional row.
    • List the names from top to bottom in the same order as they will appear on your poster from left to right.
    • Do not list yourself because we assume you are the first author.
    • Please be sure to include your mentor as appropriate.
  • In up to 300 words, describe your research in language understandable by a general audience. Before submitting, please check over your abstract one last time to ensure that special characters and formatting (e.g. subscripts, superscripts, bold text, line breaks, etc.) are properly entered/applied.
  • This field is for validation purposes and should be left unchanged.
