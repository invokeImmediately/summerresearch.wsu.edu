Summer Undergraduate Research Opportunities

Where To Find Opportunities

Looking for summer undergraduate research opportunities? Check out the following resources for finding the research project that fits you perfectly:

  • NSF Research Experiences for Undergraduates: the National Science Foundation maintains a database of the large number of research projects it funds through its Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU) program. You can search the database by subject area to find and examine opportunities and learn about who you must contact to apply to the REU you are interested in.
  • Pathways to science: a research opportunity posting board that includes a database of paid summer research and internships for undergraduate students and helpful advice on how to find and apply to research programs.
