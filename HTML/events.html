Calendar of Events and Deadlines

  • All Program Orientation & Welcome

    Date: June 1
    Locations: ETRL 119 & 101

    • 8:45 - 9:15 a.m.: Refershments (ETRL 119). Feel free to mingle and meet people.
    • 9:15: Formal program will begin in ETRL 101.

    Agenda for formal program: First thing on the agenda will be ALL introductions - students/faculty/staff etc. So please come to meet the over 60 students that will be researching this summer. Faculty are welcome to leave after introductions. Introductions will be followed by announcements, information about the summer schedule, group photo, time for individual groups to meet with their students, groups leaving for cougar card center/campus tours, etc.

  • Wednesdays on the Mall

    Dates: Every Wednesday from May 18 - July 27
    Location: Glen Terrel Mall outside the CUB

    Fun, optional event that combines BBQ with concerts. Note that only cash will be accepted at the BBQ tent, which has a weekly rotating menu. Concerts are free. More >>

  • Weekly Brown Bags

    Dates: Every Thursday
    Time: 11:00 a.m. - Noon
    Locations: will vary

    Informal lunch time meetings with a faculty representative from each of the summer programs that will talk about their research as well as their career path. ALL students are invited to attend.

  • Graduate School Information Session

    Date: Tuesday, June 14
    Time: 1:00 - 2:30 p.m.
    Location: Lighty 405

    Come and learn about graduate school and the application process.

  • "How to Write an Abstract" Workshop

    Date: Wednesday, July 6
    Time: 10:00 - 11:00 a.m.
    Location: CUE 405

    Receive training on how to write an effective abstract, perhaps the most important yet difficult to perfect element of research writing.

  • DEADLINE: Abstracts Due for Summer Poster Symposium

    Date: Wednesday, July 13

    Submission of your abstracts on or before this date is required if you plan to participate in the poster symposium.

  • "How to Make a Poster" Workshop

    Date: Wednesday, July 20
    Time: 10:00 - 11:00 a.m.
    Location: CUE 405

    This workshop will cover the key elements of an effective research poster and take you through the process of creating them.

  • Summer Poster Symposium

    Date: Tuesday, August 2
    Time: 9:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.
    Location: CUE Atrium

    Schedule for the symposium:

    • 8:15 a.m.: Students should arrive to hang their posters
    • 8:45: Group photos taken
    • 9:15: Keynote Talk/Program begins
    • 10:00: Poster symposium open to the public
