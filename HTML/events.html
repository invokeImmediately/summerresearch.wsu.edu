Upcoming Event Details

  • Wednesdays on the Mall

    Dates: Every Wednesday midday Location: Glen Terrell Mall (outside Compton Union Building--the "CUB")

    Fun, optional event that combines BBQ with casual concerts. Note that cash is required to purchase food from the BBQ tent; however, the concerts are free. Visit the Dining Services website for more information.

  • Weekly All-Program Brown Bags for Summer Researchers

    Dates: Every Thursday Time: Noon - 1:00 p.m. Locations: will varyDownload Full Schedule|Click/tap to download the complete schedule, with locations, for brown bags.

    Informal lunch meetings with faculty representatives from each of the summer programs who will talk about their research as well as their career path. ALL summer research students are invited to attend.

    June 30 (VBR 201): "Biomedicine" presented by Samantha Gizerian, Clinical Assistant Professor in Integrative Physiology and Neuroscience.

    July 7 (CUE 512): "Smart Environments" presented by Larry Holder, Professor in the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.

    July 14 (CUE 512 via AMS; VSCI 140 in WSU-Vancouver): "Landscape Ecology and Ecosystem Dynamics in the Columbia River Basin: Integrating Terrestrial and Aquatic Perspectives" presented by Gretchen Rollwagen-Bollens, Clinical Associate Professor in the School of the Environment, School of Biological Sciences.

    July 21 (CUE 512): TBD

  • "How to Write an Abstract" Workshop

    Date: Wednesday, July 6 Time: 10:00 - 11:00 a.m. Location: PACCAR 202More Info|PACCAR Environmental Technology Building
    (click/tap link to see full map)

    Receive training on how to write an effective abstract, which is perhaps the most important yet difficult-to-perfect element of research writing.

  • DEADLINE: Abstracts Due for Summer Poster Symposium

    Date: Wednesday, July 13

    Submission of your abstracts online on or before this date is required if you plan to participate in the poster symposium.

  • "How to Make a Poster" Workshop

    Date: Wednesday, July 20 Time: 10:00 - 11:00 a.m. Location: PACCAR 202More Info|PACCAR Environmental Technology Building
    (click/tap link to see full map)

    This workshop will cover the key elements of an effective research poster and take you through the process of creating one that represents your efforts well.

  • Summer Poster Symposium

    Date: Tuesday, August 2 Time: 9:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m. Location: CUE Atrium

    Schedule for the symposium:

    • 8:15 a.m.: Students should arrive to hang their posters
    • 8:45 a.m.: Group photos taken
    • 9:15 a.m.: Keynote Talk/Program begins
    • 10:00 a.m.: Poster symposium opens to the public
