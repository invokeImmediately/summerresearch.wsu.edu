Summer Research Poster Symposium

Steps to Success

  1. Work with a mentor to develop and implement a summer research project.
  2. Complete as much of the research as possible in a timely fashion.
  3. Attend the abstract writing workshop June 23 from 1-2 p.m., location TBD.
  4. Write and submit an abstract using this template to UG.Research@wsu.edu for the poster session.
    DEADLINE: 7/10/15
  5. Attend the poster design workshop from 2-3 p.m., July 15, location TBD. Poster Templates Available!
  6. Design a poster to be printed at Large Format Printing. DEADLINE: 7/22/15
  7. Pick up poster the day before the event.
  8. Get dressed up and come ready to talk about your research to experts in your field as well as the general public.

The poster symposium is the capstone experience of student researchers' summer work at WSU. It also offers opportunities to talk to fellow researchers and their guests about the wide variety of work that has been completed in 10 weeks.

This year, the summer symposium will be from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. July 31 in the Smith Center for Undergraduate Education (CUE) Atrium (second floor).

The event is open to the public and light refreshments will be served. Lunch will be provided for all student researchers.

Workshops related to the July 31 symposium

  • "How to write an abstract" -View the Workshop!
  • "How to make a poster" - July 15, 2-3 p.m., location TBD. NOTE: posters are due to the printer July 22!
To get ideas, watch last year's poster workshop; watch the video here!

Poster Costs and Templates

Your summer research program may be able to pay for your poster if you submit it by Wed. July 22 to the Large Format Print (LFP) Shop in the College of Engineering and Architecture. LFP is located in ETRL 203; someone is usually there from 8:30 a.m.-Noon, and 1-4 p.m. http://lfp.mme.wsu.edu/

Please talk to your program director about obtaining an Interdepartmental Requisition Invoice (IRI) in order to pay for the poster printing.
